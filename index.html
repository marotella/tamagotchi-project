Welcome to Simgotchi

Raise your Sim from an infant to old age, by taking care of their needs!

Directions: After clicking the button "Click Me to Play!" It will prompt you to name your sim! Once you sim is named, it will appear as a baby. Your goal is to raise your sim from toddler, kid, teen, adult, and finally old age. Each stage lasts 5 rounds, and you must make sure your Sim does not reach level 10 for hunger, sleepiness, or boredom for more than a day.
