Welcome to Simgotchi

Raise your Sim from a toddler to old age, by taking care of their needs!

Directions: After clicking the button "Click Me to Play!" It will prompt you to name your sim! Once your sim is named, it will appear as a toddler. Your goal is to raise your sim from toddler, kid, teen, adult, and finally old age. Each stage lasts 5 rounds, and you must make sure your Sim does not reach level 10 for hunger, sleepiness, or boredom for more than a day.

Take care of your sim by selecting one of the following actions.

Hunger Actions

Your sim needs to eat!

Choose to "Eat Veggies" to get a moderate decrease in hunger. You can also have your sim "Eat Junk Food" to decrease their hunger by a good bit, but be careful because they might feel a little tired.

Sleepiness Actions

Your sim needs to rest!

Choose to "Cat Nap" to get a moderate decrease in sleepiness. You can also have your sim "Deep Sleep" to significantly decrease their sleepiness, but be careful because they might wake up pretty bored.

Boredom Actions

Your sim needs to have fun!

Choose to "Watch TV" to get a moderate decrease in boredom but you might feel a little sleepy after. You can also have your sim "Go for a Hike" to significantly decrease thier boredom, but be careful because get a little sleepy and hungry from the exercise.
