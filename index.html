Welcome to Simgotchi

Raise your Sim from a toddler to old age, by taking care of their needs!

Directions: After your sim is created, it is now your responsibility to raise it from a toddler to an elder! You must make sure your Sim does not reach level 10 for hunger, sleepiness, or boredom.
However getting older is not easy! Each time your sim gets older, they get a little tired, bored, and hungry after growing up!

Take care of your sim by selecting one of the following actions.

Feed your Sim

Your sim needs to eat!

Choose to "Eat Veggies" to get a moderate decrease in hunger. You can also have your sim "Eat Junk Food" to decrease their hunger by a good bit, but be careful because they might feel a little tired.

Turn off the Lights

Your sim needs to rest!

Choose to "Cat Nap" to get a moderate decrease in sleepiness. You can also have your sim "Deep Sleep" to significantly decrease their sleepiness, but be careful because they might wake up pretty bored.

Have fun!

Your sim needs to have fun!

Choose to "Watch TV" to get a moderate decrease in boredom but they might feel a little sleepy after. You can also have your sim "Go for a Hike" to significantly decrease thier boredom, but be careful because they might get a little sleepy and hungry from the exercise.
